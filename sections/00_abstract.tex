\begin{abstract}
    The rkmh toolkit is a collection of algorithms and filtering methods recently released. It is used for the classification of reads inwith next generation sequenzing technologies. The autors of the algorith state, that it is especially useful and more acurate than thann its predecessors mash and sourmash in finding coinfections of different but similar sublinagees of viruses. They demonstrate their results for the Human papillomavirus (HPV). While presenting the result their paper lacks explaining underlying technologies. This work aims to supplemnt the original paper of the rkmh by explaining different techequies sued by the rkmh toolkit. Also the autor compares it to other alogithm and gives a brief perspective about alternative use cases.
\end{abstract}
